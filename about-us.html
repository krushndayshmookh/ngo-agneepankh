Who are we ?

Agneepankh Foundation has been unabatedly functioning in Nagpur & Islampur, since 2018.The incredible journey of our organization begins from 19th February 2018. The unswerving predilection of social welfare has kept the entire fraternity active. We have taken up the charge & has started extending quality education ,instill core competency through value based educational campaigns at destitute schools and for the students coming from indigent group of society.

Today, We are catering across state of Maharashtra having 105+ like-minded heads.We implement common culture of leadership resting on the pillar of common values that created a bedrock of highlycomitted and influential stratum.We have been fortunate to partner with some prestigious organizations to make the vision of social and emotional learning, life skill development and character strengthening among children a reality, leading to substantial benefits in the short, medium, and long term.

Our team reinforces a positive impact by integrating various ambitious projects namely “Drishti” ,”Edyou”, in order to instill in them fundamental foundation of knowledge. Forbye the academic part,we pursuits eco-friendly activities, proffer succor to flood victims which makes the journey overwhelming. All this forge existed due to a balance between methodology and team commitment for the change we wish to see.

Branches

Nagpur

It encompasses various subteams namely- outdoor,east& west teaching teams, Edyou, where all the responsibilities are gripped by our benevolent teammates. Outdoor team execute ventures which appends- Tree plantation,Spot Fixing event ,Health checkup camps(specially for disabled), Solace to flood relief , Fundraising events, Books donation drive etc. East & West teaching teams emphasis on projects namely- Project Scholarship, Project Happy weekends to assuage kids in transcending their goal. Edyou team mentors rescued girls thereby ameliorating their life. By this, volunteers slog in respective team & strive for exuberance of society.
