Happy Weekends

We set camps every Saturdays where our east & west teaching teams come together in NMC schools, teaching regular subjects to children coupling activities which promoting activity-based learning. It encompasses affable relations between volunteers & school children that makes them capable of setting foot on zenith. Tutoring & helping the students to achieve their scholastic goals in regular assessment exams is the prime responsibility and objective of the volunteer, along with teaching sessions are followed with sports activities with a motive to cover the overall objective of maintaining good health as well.

Project Drishti

Agneepankh foundation in collaboration with ‘Utkarsh association for the Blind’ took the initiative of helping the visually impaired excel in their academic career. Our volunteers mentor the impaired in achieving their desired goals. Acquaint with the skill of using Braille themselves, our volunteers enable their holistic development by helping them achieve the goals they found previously difficult or impossible to accomplish. Guidance and counseling on cruciality that they are equal and valued member of the society, We are pleased with thriving results.

An initiative taken towards the benefit of the visually impaired focusing on making them a part of mainstream education on an equal basis.

Project Scholarship

The project undertakes duties like providing mnemonics that work as an impetus for delivering excellence. A weekly perusal of the academic performance is carried & implemented of students by the volunteers in their respective camps. The quality of the result determines the caliber of their performance. Further, in order to boost their scores, we conduct mock tests, followed by the acknowledgment of their shortcomings & superseded with a suitable action plan for every student individually. The prime focus is to help students excel in their education economically.

Believing in our responsibility toward nature, we framed Project Outdoor which regards sustainability and making worthy contributions towards the environment.

Project Outdoor

We organize practices extending our gratification and taking accountability towards nature. We have previously been a part of activities like Tress plantations, cleanliness drive, distribution of better and sustainable alternatives like paper bags, etc. We aim at organizing and managing further beneficial and fun activities.

Agneepankh fun camp

This awaited joyful event lasts for a week which caters children with innovative and exciting activities for every day of the functioning camp. Activities like drawing, craft, painting, dance, singing, etc offer the kids with a forum to not only explore their talents but also flaunt it. Seminars on personality development, healthy discussions, and social events are conducted giving out resourceful outcomes. The volunteers get to harness this opportunity to be creative and explore their talents creating camaraderie among the volunteers.
