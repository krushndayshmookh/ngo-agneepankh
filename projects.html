Making Weekends productive

Happy Weekends

We set camps every Saturday and Sunday, where our East Nagpur & West Nagpur teaching teams come together in respective NMC schools, teaching regular subjects to children coupling activities which promote activity-based learning. It encompasses affable relations between the volunteers & the school children that make them capable of setting foot on zenith. Tutoring & helping the students to achieve their scholastic goals in regular assessment exams is the prime responsibility and objective of the Agneepankh volunteers. Along with the teaching sessions followed with sports activities are conducted with a motive to cover the overall objective of maintaining good health as well.

“Even without sight, there is still vision, where exists knowledge”

Project Drishti

Agneepankh foundation in collaboration with ‘Utkarsh association for the Blind’ took the initiative of helping the visually impaired girls excel in their academic career. Our volunteers mentor the impaired girls in achieving their desired goals. Acquaint with the skill of using Braille themselves, our volunteers enable their holistic development by helping them achieve the goals they found previously difficult or impossible to accomplish. Guidance and counselling on cruciality that they are equal and valued member of the society, we are pleased with thriving results.

‘We invest in the future of unseen children.”

Project Edyou

Agneepankh Foundation in association with the Zero Gravity Foundation has launched the 'Project Ed-you' by recruiting a team of competent volunteers, who took over the little angels of a shelter home. Every Sunday our volunteer base works at providing educational and mentoring support to the children. Our super-volunteers strive to make even the most vulnerable girl child realise her potential and dare to work for her dream. Organising influential seminars and discussions on enhancing life quality and well-being through radiating awareness on the importance of education, intellectual transformation and taking over sensitised social issues is a part in which the project stands for. Upgrading the academic performance of all the shelter home girls is the prime motive and responsibility of every Project volunteer.

ENABLING STUDENTS TO ACHIEVE A SCHOLARSHIP

Project Scholarship

The project undertakes duties like providing mnemonics that work as an impetus for delivering excellence. A weekly perusal of the students' academic performance is carried & implemented by the volunteers in their respective camps. The quality of the result determines the calibre of their performance. Further, in order to boost the scholastic scores, we conduct mock tests, followed by the acknowledgment of their shortcomings & superseded with a suitable action plan for every student individually. The prime focus is to help students excel in their educational hikes.

Shaping a Better Future

Project Saksham

Agneepankh foundation under guidance of IPS Lohit Maltani (DCP zone 3, Nagpur), has come up with employable skill development-cum-employable generation program which aims at uplifting 30 underprivileged youth of urban poor population in Nagpur, this includes 4 months of extensive & hybrid training carrier assistance program. This benefits in seeking employment opportunities and gain competence though emphasis on skills.

Happiness doesn't result from what we get, but from what we give.

Project Outdoor Activities

"Community well-being" is at the heart of the Outdoor Activities project. Our benevolent team organised a 'Books donation drive' thereby forming a unique component of the curriculum. 'Tree plantation drive' as geared towards land auspices. 'Health checkup camps' (especially for disabled) with a sacred aim to carry out health intervention amongst the deprived population. 'Kerela & Sangli flood relief' to prevail on natural calamities. 'Spot-fixing event', 'Aye watan event', 'Fundraising event', intending to humanitarian solace. Believing in our responsibility towards nature, We framed this project which regards sustainability and making worthy contributions towards the environment.We organize practices extending our gratification and taking accountability towards nature. We have previously been a part of activities like distribution of better and sustainable alternatives like paper bags, etc. We aim at organizing and managing further beneficial and fun activities.

Recreation at Inception of academics.

Agneepankh Fun Camp

This awaited joyful event lasts for a week which caters children with innovative and exciting activities for each day of the functioning camp. Activities like drawing, craft, painting, dance, singing, etc offer the kids with a forum to not only explore their talents but also flaunt it. Impactful seminars on personality development, brainstorming sessions and social events are conducted giving out resourceful outcomes. The volunteers get to harness this opportunity to be more creative and explore their talents creating camaraderie among themselves.
