FAQ'S

  2. Questions

Volunteer

1. How to apply?

Call us on 9595020056 or fill the registration form provided on Agneepankh Foundation’s website www.___________.com, we’ll contact you soon.

2. What tasks will be given to the newly recruit?

After getting selected in the foundation, Core team will be examining and providing you required training in induction program to allot appropriate tasks for individuals.

3. Are there any prerequisite?

Determination towards Community Care, that’s all you need.

4. Is there any joining fee?

No, you just have to submit the registration form, photograph and valid documents for identification.

5. What are we looking for, in newly recruit?

We believe that everyone is a leader and is passionately committed towards something. We look for the best in each person and use their potential constructively. Critical thinking and diversity can present us with plethora of knowledge, people and ideas.

6. What is Agneepankh Foundation’s selection process?

Applicants are evaluated and based on their forte they will be assigned teams.

7. Will we be getting paid?

No, Agneepankh Foundation is a Non-Profitable organization, members volunteer solely because they wish to work for society.

8. Can someone here convince my parents?

Yes. At first, we at Agneepankh Foundation will try and assign work as per volunteer’s convenience, nevertheless, if field work is needed, we assure to persuade.

9. Why should I join Agneepankh Foundation?

One must join only with a will to serve, there indeed is scope of personal development. We get to learn social, business, interpersonal and network skills. You get to understand different strata of society and their needs. Along with all these we provide you the platform and certification.

10. Will we be trained before I start my work?

Yes, newly recruits will be examined and provided training program by core team leads. And based on their potential tasks will be allotted.

11. If I don’t get selected now, can I re-apply later?

Yes, as long as you are willing to learn and improve you are more than welcome.

12. What are the working hours?

Working hours are flexible, field work is mostly scheduled on Saturdays and Sundays and public holidays.

13. When will I get to know whether I am selected or not?

Mostly, HR department will get back to you in a day or two, but if you still face problem you can directly contactus.

14. Can I contribute in Agneepankh Foundation through backend activities?

You can contribute by assisting:
  • Human resource team
  • Helping Database team
  • Social media management strategy designing
  • Graphic designing
  • Content creation
  • Curriculum planning, and preparing study material for teaching team
  • Impact research team
  • And many more

15. I am not from nagpur,how can I contribute to the organisation in any way?

The same answer as to the above Question.

16. If I left in future, are there any policies and benefits for Alumni?

Alumni are our treasure and Agneepankh Foundation will be in contact with us, there presence will always be encouraged.

17. If I left in future, are there any policies and benefits for Alumni?

Alumni are our treasure and Agneepankh Foundation will be in contact with us, there presence will always be encouraged.

Internship

1. Do you provide internship?

Yes, if you wish to apply the do fill the registration form available on our website. (Join US)

2. Are we providing stipend?

No

3. What if I don’t fulfil internship terms?

We will check for the reason; only genuine reason will be taken under consideration else it will be considered as a violation of internship declaration. In this case, intern will be terminated and no benefit will be provided for the same.
